About Us

Sadhana is a musical initiative to support tabla players by providing curated lehra sound tracks in various taals and raags. Our aim is to centralize quality lehras so that students and professionals alike can enhance their practice sessions. Every lehra on this site is selected with care to match the rhythmic cycles essential for tabla performance.
